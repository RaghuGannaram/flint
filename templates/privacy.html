Privacy Policy

Welcome to Flint. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data while using our platform.

🔒 Information We Collect

We collect various types of data to improve our services and provide a better user experience:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, profile picture, and other details when you register.
  • Review Data: Product reviews, ratings, and feedback you submit.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and access logs.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies to remember preferences and improve platform performance.

📌 How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide platform functionality (e.g., displaying reviews, managing accounts).
  • Enhance security by detecting suspicious activities and preventing fraud.
  • Personalize recommendations and optimize content for a better user experience.
  • Improve Flint's features based on analytics and user behavior.
  • Communicate with users for updates, newsletters, and customer support.

🔍 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Flint uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience. Cookies help us:

  • Remember user preferences and login sessions.
  • Analyze site traffic and improve content recommendations.
  • Serve personalized advertisements (only with user consent).

You can manage your cookie preferences from your browser settings.

🔄 Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

We do not sell or trade your personal data. However, we may share necessary data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, security) to maintain Flint’s functionality.
  • Law enforcement if required by law or to protect user safety.
  • Third-party analytics tools (Google Analytics, etc.) to understand user behavior.

🛡️ Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse. Our security measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive user data.
  • Secure authentication mechanisms.
  • Regular security audits and monitoring.

🛠️ Your Rights & Choices

As a user of Flint, you have the following rights:

  • Access your data: View what data we collect.
  • Edit or delete your data: Modify or remove your account details anytime.
  • Opt-out of marketing emails: Unsubscribe from newsletters or promotional content.
  • Disable cookies: Adjust browser settings to restrict cookies.

📞 Contact & Questions

📄 Policy Updates

Flint may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and significant updates will be communicated to users.
